General Questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at HOOK/TURNS is not required if you only want to access the 'Next Tram' and 'Route Guide', BUT it is required when using "Tram Condition" for verification purposes. Rest assured that all your information would be secretly shared with all other interested third parties.

How can I check tram crowededness level?

You can go to NexTram, search for particular tram number, and view the crowdedness level
